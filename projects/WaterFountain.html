Water Fountain for a Cat

This project involved designing a DIY water fountain using Arduino some simple electronics, this is both efficient and fun!

Why I Built This: I decided to create a water fountain specifically for my cat after noticing that many cats are not fond of drinking water from stagnant sources. Recognizing the importance of hydration for feline health, I took it upon myself to design and build a unique water fountain tailored to my cat's preferences. The flowing water not only adds an element of freshness but also appeals to my cat's natural instincts. The custom water fountain not only serves the practical purpose of encouraging my cat to drink more water, promoting overall well-being, but it also provides an aesthetically pleasing addition to the living space. It's a small yet thoughtful way to enhance my pet's daily life and ensure they stay adequately hydrated, contributing to their health and happiness.

Materials Needed:

  • Arduino UNO Board
  • Mini DIY Water Pump
  • WiFi Shield
  • Model-X Motor Driver
  • Ultrasonic Sensor Module
  • Cables

Estimated Cost: Around $60 USD.
